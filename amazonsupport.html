F.A.Q. and Support

1. About Our Amazing Games

1. What kind of games do you provide in this app?

This app is dedicated to best and most interesting classic and favorite logical and table games. Whether you are in your home, office, cafe or any of your favorite places you can always enjoy the best of old-favorite games and even more!

2. Will this list of games be updated over time?

Of course! We are always eager to provide you the most exciting games so we regularly update and we constantly improve the catalogs of available games in every section

2. About Playing the Games in the App

1. What are levels and how can I level-up?

The more your level is the more games you can play in the app. Leveling-up is also really simple. Just choose one or more your favorite games and keep playing them. Every hand played in every game increases your level progress and brings your closes to the level-ups with the new cool games!

2. What should I do if I want to play games without Internet connection?

With our app it is really simple! You can put any of the available games into an app cache for offline use so you can access and play them any time completely without Internet connection. To do so just open the game and select the Download button located in the upper right of the screen. You can also remove your cached games to free some space, just open My Cached Games option in your Profile
